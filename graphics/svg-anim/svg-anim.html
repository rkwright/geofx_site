SVG Animation

These pages are a set of demos of using SVG animation, both legacy declarative, JavaScript and newer CSS3 animation. Each demo consists of a brief overview of the nimations methods used, followed by the animation itself. Just click on the thumbnails to read the overview and see the demos.

A declarative SVG demo of the patterns of population growth in the United States over the last 200 years.
