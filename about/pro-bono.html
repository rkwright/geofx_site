Pro Bono Activities


In his spare time Geo-F/X's principal, Ric Wright, also serves on the board of a non-profit organization, The Friends of Moraine Hills State Park, that helps to support a local regional park. The organization serves to raise additional funds for the park, raises native gardens, helps with native landscape restoraton and more. In addition to serving the board and other volunteer work, Ric also maintains the group's website, Friends of Moraine Hills State Park

One of the fund-raisiing activities that "The Friends" support is the Moraine Hills Run for the Hills 5k, 10k and half marathon run in the park. Ric also maintains that site. He also lends a hand when needed at a third site, the Friends of Volo Bog.
